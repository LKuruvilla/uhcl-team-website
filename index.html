University of Houston - Clear Lake
VeriScan is an android application that helps scan receipt and stores the data in user's google drive account. Users can login to the application using their google credentials take an image of their receipt and voice tag the image before storing it in google drive. The application will correctly identify the type of receipt, it's content and store the data in a spreadsheet along with a link to the image in a google spreadsheet. The reciepts are sorted according to their type, date, month and year. The application can also work with e-mailed receipts providing user a way to process recripts and to store them in their google drive account.
