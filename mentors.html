Ravi Ganta, CEO

Mr. Ravi Ganta is a technology entrepreneur and a leader in enterprise software engineering. He is the founder and director of Mindagile.com, a software technology consulting firm based in Houston, TX that builds innovative technology solutions for its clients. He also serves as a technology advisor / technical co-founder on multiple B2B and B2C start-up companies. He has over 15 years of experience in architecting and building enterprise software in Media, Energy, Government, and Life Sciences domains. He is passionate about the exponential potential of technology in improving lives, and works to realize it through various entrepreneurial ventures. He believes that teaching is the best way to learn, so he takes time to teach high school kids Math and Computer Science, and also offers various Capstone projects to Computer Science graduate students at University of Houston-Clear Lake.
